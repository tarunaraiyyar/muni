The Problem

The Daily
Strain

"It's just… my day-to-day commute shouldn't feel like survival, you know?" — Muni Rider Interview

How might we redesign an experience that gives commuters more comfort and control, without losing the civic spirit of public transportation?

Finding

Mood is lower during the commute than any other daily activity

Finding

Reliability, cleanliness, and crowding are the biggest satisfaction gaps

Experience Blueprint

What We
Heard

01

Comfort as Control

"I like the bus when it's empty; I can listen to music and just be calm." Riders don't want luxury — they want agency over how they feel during the ride.

02

Crowding Breeds Stress

"I don't know whether it's going to be a rowdy group of kids or an aggressive person." Overcrowding triggers anxiety and aggression, degrading the shared experience.

03

Uncertainty Creates Anxiety

"If there are too many people, it gets loud and stressful. I just wait for the next one." Not knowing seat availability forces riders to sacrifice time for peace of mind.

04

Equity Is Non-Negotiable

"Public transport should feel fair — not like some people get comfort and others don't." Any solution must preserve civic spirit, not create a two-tier underclass.

Current State of the Art

No Middle
Ground

Every current option trades off either comfort or equity — there's no middle ground between public accessibility and personal control.

✓ Smooth & quieter ride

✗ Limited coverage & last-mile access

Personal Transit

✓ Full control & privacy

✗ Costly, parking stress, traffic delays

Rideshare

✓ Convenient & on-demand

✗ Expensive, isolates riders

The Concept

Introducing
MUNI+

An upgrade to Muni that enables calm and predictable journeys through personalized seat selection and improved buses.

🎫

The Pass

A Clipper-integrated Muni+ pass. Monthly at $108/mo or pay-per-ride at $3.85 — minimal friction, maximum access.

Access Layer
📱

The App

Smart seat booking with real-time route intelligence. Reserve your seat before you leave home. Get arrival notifications. Check in with a tap.

Control Layer
🚌

The Bus

Retrofitted Muni+ coaches with improved seating, lighting, and designated zones — built on existing fleet infrastructure for low-cost rollout.

Experience Layer

User Flow

The Journey

1

Discovery & Sign-Up

2

What's Next

Phased
Rollout

01
Year 1

Pilot Launch

Partner with SFMTA to retrofit select buses with Muni+ interior upgrades and integrate seat booking into the existing Muni app. Validate desirability and technical feasibility on a high-demand commuter route.

02
Year 2

Commuter Corridors

Introduce Muni+ service to 5–6 additional peak-hour routes. Launch corporate subscription partnerships. Test scalability and revenue generation while improving commuter access.

03
Year 3–4

Citywide Integration

Fully integrate Muni+ tiers into the Clipper system and deploy consistent design standards across all upgraded buses. Establish Muni+ as a self-sustaining premium tier.
